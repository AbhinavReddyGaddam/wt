Recipes

1. Veg Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Tomato, cucumber, onion (sliced)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on the bread slices.
  2. Layer vegetables on one slice.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper.
  4. Cover with the other slice and serve.

2. Quick Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled pasta
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Chopped onion, tomato, capsicum
  • Salt, chili flakes, oregano

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté vegetables.
  2. Add boiled pasta and spices.
  3. Mix well and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Serve hot.

3. Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon
  • 1 glass cold water
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze lemon juice into a glass.
  2. Add sugar and salt, stir well.
  3. Pour water and mix.
  4. Serve chilled.
